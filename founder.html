The passion behind it

Why I built this

I'm Siyamthanda, and like every Rhodes student I've spent late nights scrolling chaotic WhatsApp groups trying to find a cheap textbook, sell a fridge before moving res, or figure out a safe way home from town. I watched friends get scammed. I watched bursary deadlines fly past unnoticed. I watched people walk home alone because a cab felt like a gamble.

It bothered me that we — a tight-knit campus full of brilliant, resourceful people — didn't have one place that was truly ours. So I decided to build it. Not as a business chasing commission, but as an initiative for the community I'm part of and care deeply about.

Campus Marketplace is my love letter to Rhodes students: a place to trade fairly, get home safely, and stay connected to every opportunity Makhanda has to offer. Uncle Ross the Rhodent is here to make it feel like home. 💜
