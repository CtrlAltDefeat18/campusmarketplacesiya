Our story

Built in Makhanda.
For Makhanda. 🇿🇦

Campus Marketplace started with a simple frustration: students were buying and selling in scattered, chaotic WhatsApp groups, getting scammed, missing out on cheaper textbooks, and walking home alone at night. There had to be a better way — something that felt like ours.

So we built one. A single, friendly home for the Rhodes University & Makhanda community where you can trade safely, get home safely, and never miss a bursary deadline again. We don't process payments and we don't take a cut — we just connect people who already share a campus.
